University of Iowa

编辑



The University of Iowa (also known as the UI, U of I, or simply Iowa) is a public research university in Iowa City, Iowa. Founded in 1847, Iowa is the oldest university in the state. The University of Iowa is the flag ship university for the state of Iowa. The University of Iowa is organized into eleven colleges offering more than 200 areas of study and seven professional degrees.
The Iowa campus spans 1,700 acres centered along the banks of the Iowa River and includes the University of Iowa Hospitals and Clinics, named one of "America Best Hospitals" for the 25th year in a row. The university was the original developer of the Master of Fine Arts degree and it operates the world-renowned Iowa Writers Workshop. Iowa has very high research activity, and is a member of several research coalitions, including the prestigious Association of American Universities, the Universities Research Association, and the Committee on Institutional Cooperation. The Iowa alumni network exceeds 250,000, and the university budgeted revenues and expenses of $3.513 billion for 2015.
The University of Iowa's athletic teams, the Hawkeyes, compete in Division I of the NCAA and are members of the Big Ten Conference. The Hawkeyes field 24 varsity teams and have won 27 national championships.

UI是一所典型美国top100中部州立大学，除了个别学科排名拔尖如医学院，法学院，文学等中国人读的很少的学科，其他学科和排名差不多（上下浮动20名）的大学都没有太多区别。商学院本科一般，因为地理原因，每年找到实习工作的中国学生凤毛麟角，大多数同学(个人感觉85%以上?)毕业选择继续去大城市深造或者回国工作。文理学院也差不多，除了电脑cs，精算相对好找一点（和美国大环境需求一样），读其他的科目的同学一般都会继续读下去，工科情况也算一般，不好不坏。

学校中国学生会每年基本上会组织一次中国学生篮球赛和其他小球比赛。学校体育设施也还不错，有几个大的体育馆，其中rec center在全美大学体育馆也名列前茅。学校体育部每年也会有校级比赛（篮球橄榄球足球乒乓球羽毛球……）供大家参加。iowa city有soccer league提供给足球爱好者联赛的机会，中国学生教工有组织球队并且每年名次都非常不错，有兴趣的新生可以主动去球场寻找。
天气很中部，雪从十一月下到来年四月。

最后想说的是UI综合下来其实还不错，如果你没有sat成绩的话会是一个不错的选择。
作为一个刚毕业的校友也是蛮惆怅感慨这几年的时光的，好的坏的都有，但是 Once a hawkeye, always a hawkeye！

Question:

Which of the three is better for bachelors in computer science, University of Iowa, Arizona State University or University of Buffalo?
I have admit from all the three universities, for bachelors in CS.

I will be an international student if I take the admit, I was wondering which of the three is better ? In terms of studies, faculties and research. Also in terms of type of job, co-op and internships I can get at each of these.
Answer:
It doesn't really matter that much for which one to go to since they all seem to be on a same/similar level: all are public school and possibly similar tuition and none of them is a really top one. With a CS degree, it also just comes down to your abilities for a job where you will be required for doing the technical interview.

What does matter is the availability of local job opportunities after you graduate. You may think you can relocate for a job, but it's way better if you can find internship at a local company you really want to work at which is located close to where your university is WHILE you are still in college.

But if you get a scholarship from either one of those schools, I would suggest you go for that one.
