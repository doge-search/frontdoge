编辑



Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute , or RPI, is a private research university located in Troy, New York, with two additional campuses in Hartford and Groton, Connecticut. It was founded in 1824 by Stephen van Rensselaer and Amos Eaton for the "application of science to the common purposes of life" and is described as the oldest technological university in the English-speaking world. Built on a hillside, RPI's 265-acre (107 ha) campus overlooks the city of Troy and the Hudson River and is a blend of traditional and modern architecture. The institute operates an on?campus business incubator and the 1,250-acre (510 ha) Rensselaer Technology Park. Numerous American colleges or departments of applied sciences were modeled after Rensselaer. The university is one among a small group of polytechnic universities in the United States which tend to be primarily devoted to the instruction of technical arts and applied sciences.
Rensselaer is organized into six main schools within which there are thirty-seven departments: the School of Architecture; School of Engineering; School of Humanities, Arts, and Social Sciences; School of Information Technology and Web Science; School of Science; and the Lally School of Management & Technology. The university offers around one hundred forty degree programs in sixty fields leading to bachelor's, master's, and doctorate degrees.

伦斯勒理工学院是一所在工程学、自然科学和商学方面十分拔尖的学院，坐落于一 非常美丽的地区。学校在学习技巧的广泛应用方面享有盛名，并为学生提高非常不错的实习计划安排，同时学校允许学生在较短的时间内取得本科和硕士学位。如果您正在寻找一所在工程学或科学方面实力很强的学校的话，那么Rpi就是一个非常不错的选择。目前学校正尝试招收更多的女学生，所以如果以上条件正合你意，而且你是女生的话Rpi的确是一个极好的选择。
据《菲斯克大学入学指南》报道：“伦斯勒理工学院是在工程技术领域最有实力的大学之一，其他同类学校包括加州理工学院、麻省理工学院、伍斯特理工学院和哈维姆德学院。伦斯勒理工学院的独特之处就在于它能够为学生提供将技术和管理结合起来的实践机会。”相关评论提到的该校强势专业包括电子媒体与艺术、机械工程、电子工程、核工程及计算机游戏和建筑学等。在美国国家大学排名中，Rpi名列第41位。
伦斯勒理工学院是一所小型学校，有近5400名本科生，学校另外还有2000多名研究生。正因为学校规模小，本科生才可以更多地和教授进行交流、探讨。学校的较小规模也保证了能向学生及时提供他们所需的帮助和支持。例如，《普林斯顿评论》提到学习辅导中心有一个提前侦查系统，它可以在学生成绩低于年均水平时予以警告，并且提供相应的帮助。一旦它发现学生的表现有所退步，便会就这个问题与学生进行联系交流。
伦斯勒理工学院位于纽约州北部地区的一个小镇里。小镇环境很不错，风景十分秀丽，但是可能没有太多好玩的东西，并且比较闭塞。总的来说，这里的人都很友好、好客，不过冬天可能会很冷。
伦斯勒理工学院是一所私立学校，经济来源是学费和赠款。学校被分成不同的院系，学生可以直接申请进入这些院系。不过每个院系都有其很严格的毕业要求。
伦斯勒理工学院的校园文化是由其自身的学习强度和学生比例（70%男生，30%女生）所决定的。我想大部分对工程学、自然科学或商务、科技怀有热情的中国学生都会喜欢这里。尽管如此，这所学校还是有些文化值得学生及其家长的关注，列举如下：
1，学术性强。所有评论报道都指出，这里的学生格外努力，除了上课外，他们还会为学业花费很多时间。
2，浓厚的希腊式文化氛围，几乎40%的男生和18%的女生都参加了兄弟会或者姐妹会这样的团体联谊会。这样一来，这些联谊会在整个社交体系中起了主导作用。如巜普林斯顿评论》所描述的：“到了周末，所有男生要么去兄弟会，要么留在宿舍里玩电子游戏。”
3，72%的男生。由于学校在工程学、商业课程和自然科学方面出类拔萃，所以更容易受到男生的喜爱。
4，对冰球运动的喜爱。学校没有一项强势的体育运动传统，也许是因为这里的学生大都是喜欢电子游戏和整日沉迷于计算机的“呆子”吧。不过学校在国内名列前茅的冰球队在校园文化中还是占据了一定的位置，这也是冬季最好的集体活动。

Question:

What is your review of Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute?
Answer:
Academically, it's hard to find a better school. The curriculum of courses is very in-depth, forcing a solid understanding and knowledge base. However, the challenging content combined with professors with strong accents and/or are more focused with their own research can cause a lot of stress among students.

Socially, RPI is not well balanced. The male to female ratio is about 2:1, and a large portion of the student body does not like to be involved with campus life. That said, 29 fraternities and 5 sororities create a strong, diverse Greek culture. Combined with the addition of many student clubs, there's plenty of opportunities to find like-minded people, just not always a lot of them.

As a whole, RPI is a phenomenal choice for someone who has a strong idea of what field (usually engineering related) he or she wants to pursue. On the other hand, an unprepared prospect will probably not enjoy the stay nearly as much.

Question:

What are the best reasons to attend Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute
Answer:
I attended RPI and graduated in 2007 with an ECSE major. The campus itself is a bit isolated, and most of the students hang out on/near campus than off campus. The ratio is definitely leaning more towards male than female (although I hear it has gotten better since even I graduated).

Education wise RPI is amazing.

The school has a big focus on undergraduate education and the professors try very hard to make time for their students. They definitely know who's working for their grades and who's not. I, on several occasions, emailed professors and found them to be very willing to make time to explain concepts that I was struggling with.

The career services office has all the things you would expect--resume building workshops, resume critiquing, career counseling, mock interviews, etc.

The writing center has proof reading, grammar, and so forth for any writing samples you have for any class on campus. You can be asking for help on a Ph. D. dissertation or even a simple writing assignment for an undergraduate class. I have used them before and found them helpful.

TA's are usually very knowledgable and helpful. So are the tutoring sessions offered in the Union.

I would recommend the school for any prospective student. I have never interviewed with a company that never heard of RPI to this day.
