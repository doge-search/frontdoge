编辑



Indiana University Bloomington (abbreviated "IU Bloomington" and colloquially referred to as "IU" or simply "Indiana") is a public research university located in Bloomington, Indiana, United States. With over 40,000 students, IU Bloomington is the flagship institution of the Indiana University system and its largest university.
It is a member of the Association of American Universities and has numerous schools and programs the comprise part of IU, including the Jacobs School of Music, the IU School of Informatics and Computing, the Kelley School of Business, the School of Public Health, the School of Nursing, the School of Public and Environmental Affairs, the Maurer School of Law, the IU School of Library and Information Science, and the IU School of Education.
With a Fall 2014 total campus enrollment of 42,634 students, IU Bloomington is the largest university campus in the state. While 55.2% of the student body was from Indiana, students from 49 of the 50 states, Washington D.C., and 165 foreign nations were also enrolled. The university is home to an extensive student life program, with about 17 percent of undergraduates joining the Greek system. Indiana athletic teams compete in Division I of the NCAA and are known as the Indiana Hoosiers. The university is a member of the Big Ten Conference.
Among IU Bloomington many graduate-level programs are the Kelley School of Business, School of Education and Maurer School of Law. Indiana law school has a program with a first-year team-based approach, a diversion from the typical legal education. Notable Indiana alumni include composer and songwriter Hoagy Carmichael, who penned Georgia on My Mind; John Chambers, executive chairman and former CEO of Cisco Systems; and Mark Cuban, billionaire owner of the Dallas Mavericks, Landmark Theatres, and Magnolia Pictures.
In terms of academics and other criteria, IU Bloomington ranks in top 100 national universities in the United States and the top 50 public universities in the country. The school's sports teams are notorious competitors in the NCAA Division I Big Ten Conference, and, since Indiana University does not have a mascot, all teams are known simply as Hoosiers. There are more than 650 student organizations on campus, and more than 5,000 students go Greek in the school large community of fraternities and sororities.

学术优势
　　印地安那大学伯明顿分校凯利商学院（Kelly School）始终被美国商界认定为最佳的商学教学机构。之所以能成为美国商学院的翘楚，在于其擅用与商界的关系与资源，并且提供自己的学生多种不同的见习机会。比如说，Kelly School与校外厂商合作推行的计划称为Kelly商业行政谘商〈Kelly Executive Partners〉，简称KEP。KEP计划是Kelly School的附属商业机构，负责Kelly School对外界厂商的人才培训计划，帮助厂商训练行政管理人才或评估商业行为的可能风险。KEP计划在Kelly School中已行之有年，自1952年至今，KEP计划已经为不少美国大厂提供过了商务咨询与规划。

薛天禄，2010 ~ 2011
IUB 的 HCID Program 属于典型的 third wave HCI program, 这除了由我们教授擅长和感兴趣的领域决定之外，还和本 program 成立时间较短有关（10年左右）。关于 three waves of HCI 分别是哪三波，可以参考懒人有禅的这篇文章［1］。一般来说，我们 program 希望把学生培养成 user experience designer/researcher, 而不是 usability engineer，当然，我们其中一个教授更希望我们最终进化为 design strategist, 而不是 just UX designer. 这从每个学生在入学前地暑假都被要求读完 design thinking 的代表作
Change By Design
［2］可见一斑。
我主要介绍下第一学期的三门课：
Foundation of HCI, 如其名，阐述 HCI 这门学科的基石。从 Fitt's Law［1］说到 Ubiquitous Computing ［2］, 阅读大量的学术论文（大概至少 40 篇上下），是理论课。教授风趣幽默，旁证博引，并夹杂大量学界八卦；
Interaction Design Methods, 由 Feminist HCI 大牛 Shaowen Bardzell 教，基本涵盖目前 HCI 界主流用户研究方法和设计理念，理论与实践结合；
Interaction Design Practice, 最有意思也是最难的一门课，一学期共六个 project，每个 project 从简单到复杂，涵盖设计的各个方面，都是以小组为单位的。每个 project 都会有二年级 mentor 指导，都以 presentation 结束。在这个神奇的课程中还会有 Google 的早期员工来讲课，去教堂听 pipe organ［5］等奇妙体验，而这些还不包括教授本身给我们带来的如洗礼般的设计教育，是终身难忘的课程。
另一个大牛 Eli Blevis 的 Meaning and Form in HCI 也是我个人非常喜欢的一门课。二年级以贯穿全年的 capstone （毕业设计）为主线，同时穿插很多更深层次的课程。同时和大多数美国大学一样，全学校的其他课程信息都是对学生公开，并且在时间允许的情况下可以选修和旁听的。另外也会有很多参加学术会议的机会，IxDA 和 CHI 时都会有很多学生和 faculty 参加比赛，论文投稿等。
