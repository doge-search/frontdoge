North Carolina State University

编辑



North Carolina State University, is a public, coeducational, research university located in Raleigh, North Carolina, United States. the university is part of the University of North Carolina system and is a Morrill Land-Grant University, the only NC member of the National Space Grant College and Fellowship Program, and a member of the National Space Grant College and Fellowship Program. The university forms one of the corners of the Research Triangle together with Duke University in Durham and The University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
The North Carolina General Assembly founded the North Carolina College of Agriculture and Mechanic Arts, now NC State, on March 7, 1887, as a land-grant college. Today, NC State has an enrollment of more than 34,000 students, making it the largest university in the Carolinas. NC State has historical strengths in engineering, statistics, agriculture, life sciences, textiles and design and now offers 106 bachelor's degrees. The graduate school offers 104 master's degrees, 61 doctoral degrees, and a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine.

Question:

Why should someone go to North Carolina State University for a masters degree in CS?
What would be some really good reasons to join North Carolina State University for a masters program?
Answer:
It really depends on what you want to focus on. NC State has some of the best professors for Systems, Networks and Machine Learning. Dr. Injong Rhee, Dr. George Rouskas are pioneers in the field of Networks. Dr. Rhee's contribution is the CUBIC congestion algorithm which is used in almost 40% of the linux servers in the world. Dr. Rouskas works on Optical Networks and Network Virtualization. With SDN catching up, it's an exciting time to work under him. For systems, there is Dr. Frank Mueller who researches in a variety of fields from Parallel computing, GPGPU parallelism and High Performance Computing. He has one of the most powerful clusters in the triangle. And, there is Dr. Nagiza Samatova who is known for her research in weather prediction studies.

Of course, there are several other professors who have some really core research grants under their sleeves. Dr. Xuxian Jiang is known for his frequent contributions to Android security. Dr. Mihail Sichitu, known for his research in Wireless networks.

While there are better universities in the US when you look at streams like theoritical computer science, NC State is definitely one of the best universities for Networks and Systems.

Aside from CS, NC State has some really good professors for Electrical Engineering. There are courses like ASIC verification which are offered only in a handful of universities in the world.

- Short commutes (come home for lunch if I choose)
- Housing cost and availability (live in the area where you want to live without a soul crushing mortgage/rent payment every month)
- People, but not crowded
- Mild short winters, long springs, long autumns, (but hot in July & August)
- Variety of terrains (mountains to sandy beaches, but no deserts)
- Plenty of water (if we can keep the coal waste out of it)
- Low crime rate

- Good economy
