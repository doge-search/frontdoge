Brandeis University is an American private research university in Waltham, Massachusetts, 9 miles (14 km) west of Boston.
It was founded in 1948 as a non-sectarian Jewish community-sponsored coeducational institution on the site of the former Middlesex University. The university is named after Louis Brandeis, the first Jewish Justice of the U.S Supreme Court. In 2015 it had a total enrollment of 5,532 students on its suburban campus spanning over 235 acres. The institution offers more than 43 majors and 46 minors, and two thirds of the undergraduate classes have 20 students or less. It is a member of Association of American Universities since 1985 and the Boston Consortium which allows students to cross-register to attend courses at other institutions including Boston College, M.I.T and Tufts University.
The university has a strong liberal arts focus and a quarter of its students coming from outside the United States. It was tied for 34th among national universities in the United States in U.S. News & World Report 2016 rankings. Forbes listed Brandeis as 36th nationally for research and 37th for entrepreneurship. Times ranks it 185th globally while USA Today ranks it among the top 10 in the country for economics. The university is also home to the Heller School, one of the top 10 policy schools in America. Its alumni include Academy Award winner Michael Sugar, Pulitzer Prize winner Thomas Friedman, Nobel Prize laureate Roderick MacKinnon, former Icelandic prime minister Geir Haarde and two MacArthur Fellows.

Brandeis是一所很小的私立大学，比较注重本科的文理教育，因此文理学院是学校最大的学院，像近几年比较热门的也是规模比较大的工程学院等Brandeis是没有的。所以从整体来看Brandeis更像是liberal arts college的性质。
在Brandeis学业方面最大的好处是小班授课，我目前大一所以也不了解以后专业课的情况，但是对于大一大二这种通识教育课来说每个班的人数都比较少。写作课数学课还有一些其他的课一个班是有十几个人左右，经济这种比较热门的inroductutary课会达到七八十人左右，但据我所知还没有课会超过100人，比一些美国州立大学或大型私立学校一节课动辄三四百人一起上的情况好多了。
还有就是教授授课的比率非常高，TA教课比较少。我目前上的课全部是professor授课，没有出现TA来代讲的情况。可以保证与教授的互动。
然后就是生活方面。Brandeis所处的位置离Boston开车不堵车的话大概20分钟以内就可以到市中心。学校周末会有班车往返学校和城里但时间比较长，因为还会到Havard那边绕一下。以后如果自己有车的话进城还是很方便的。
学校周围有一家比较大的超市，还有CVS和银行什么的，总之基本的生活需求都可以满足。学校每天都有班车路过学校附近这些地方，自己走路也能到。
Brandeis的伙食真的不好…这个不是黑 我是真的说出了大家的心声，食堂有两个但都比较难吃，早餐还好。不过学校也有卖当日做好的寿司还有现做的burrito什么的，实在不行附近也有几家中餐厅送外卖，所以也不至于太惨。
总之我觉得Brandeis是一个非常适合学习的学校，周围没有太乱或者太多东西分心，但又没有村到进一次城要一两个小时这种，周末去玩也很方便。
