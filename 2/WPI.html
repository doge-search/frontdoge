Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI) is a private research university in Worcester, Massachusetts, focusing on the instruction and research of technical arts and applied sciences.
Founded in 1865 in Worcester, WPI was one of the United States' first engineering and technology universities and now has 14 academic departments with over 50 undergraduate and graduate degree programs in science, engineering, technology, management, the social sciences, and the humanities and arts, leading to bachelor's, master's and PhD degrees. WPI's faculty works with students in a number of research areas, including biotechnology, fuel cells, information security, materials processing, and nanotechnology.

刘兆晨，Google程序员，伍斯特理工2012毕业生。
我是伍斯特理工大学的本科生，12年毕业的。 就业形式不错。 原因如下：
1. 我校在波士顿区域还是不错的，很多大公司都会来我们学校招生。 在各大公司中也有不少校友。
2. 教课比较注重实践，会让我们做不少projects, 都学到很多实用的东西。
3. 遗憾的是，学校附近那真是无聊， 所以你除了学习也干不了其他什么事情。。。

topic:在伍斯特理工学院(Worcester Polytechnic Institute)就读是怎样一番体验？

answer_num:13

鱼鱼鱼，管理，运营，金融，会计
谢邀。校园很小，很像是当地的某个high的规模，记得某个学姐开玩笑说：绕着学校的笑fountain走一圈就把学校逛了一圈。麻雀虽小五脏俱全，老师与设施配备都很给力。
本科就读在EWU，感觉图书馆很大，也很空，final的时候才会见到难得多的人，但WPI图书馆经常找不到位置。快final的时候，我会在图书馆坚持到11点半，为了免费的cookie和咖啡。
说句实话，WPI学风更为严谨务实，我在WPI读商科的master，班上大部分人都是附近公司的中层亦或者是有长时间工作经历的人，大部分人都拥有engineering degree，因此思路非常清晰与想法也非常务实与创新，与他们group work，会让人成长很快，他们会告诉你，很多cases在现实中哪里会有缺失，哪里会需要加强，甚至于，将他们公司的现行问题作为研究方向，在去他们公司多次调研后，进行问题解决，与他们多接触，或许会有实习推荐机会。
学校的社团也蛮给力，WPIMBA是一个全国联盟的女性MBA组织，每年会有一些机会跟很多公司女高管一起开会，内部也会设置招聘会之类的，对实习和就业来说也还算是蛮不错的。当年我们还获得了全美学生分部奖（共设学生部与职业部两分支）。抛开这些社团，学校本身对学生就业给与的支持力度也是蛮大，CC会帮忙修改简历，招聘模拟，开设招聘会（会有至少100所公司前来招聘）。
WPI算是我学生生涯最后一站，我已回国，却一直以WPI校友身份为荣。

Question:

What is the most popular major at Worcester Polytechnic Institute? Why?
Answer:
According to facts published on the WPI website regarding student enrollment, the following data represents the most popular student majors (For Full-Time Degree Seeking Undergraduates in the year 2015):

Mechanical Engineering: 948

Computer Science: 476

Biomedical Engineering: 397

Chemical Engineering: 356

These four majors represent 2177 students out of a total of 4085 enrolled

This data was found on thier website at the following link:

Institutional Research

Regarding the reason why these are the most popular majors, I'm not sure there is any one correct answer. The programs are not capped and this is what the students have decided to study for various reasons probably including but not limited to job outlook, starting salaries, department funding/professors/research, the list goes on and on. Without a survey combined with analysis as the the reasons why, any answer to the question is speculation.
